About Me

NAME: Dan O'Neil
PRONOUNS: he/him/his (what's this?)
HOMETOWN: Chagrin Falls, OH
LOCATION: Philadelphia, PA
EDUCATION: Coding Boot Camp, University of Pennsylvania
BFA Theatre, The University of the Arts

I’ve always been drawn to crossword puzzles and logic games, and in high school I started learning to code using Microsoft Visual Basic and Java. I moved to Philadelphia in 2006 to study theatre, and worked in the arts as a director and creative producer for over a decade. Now, I’m ready to bring a ton of practical skills from that career like project planning and management, collaborative problem solving & creative thinking to a career as a full stack web developer.

I'm enrolled in a full stack web development boot camp, so I have experience in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and the Bootstrap Framework, and I'm learning new skills and languages with each project!

When I’m not behind a computer screen, you can find me at a drag show, singing with friends around a piano, or showing someone pictures of my nieces.
